Meet

Akshat Tiwari

The sharpest content ninja at BeerBiceps

All through my engineering degree years, I’ve been an intern at BeerBiceps. The only difference is that in my first year, I edited memes of my friends and by the time I graduated, I’d edited 1500+ videos for 100+ brands including Amazon, Flipkart, Netflix, Ajio, among others. Dream degree, isn’t it?

Post graduating, over the years of working as a full time editor, I’ve edited videos across genres like infotainment, vlogs, podcasts, reels, etc and thus discovered unique styles and techniques for each.

This journey has also made me realize how editing is much more than just technical skills, it’s also about the way you land on ideas to go viral and how you convey emotions through your edits.

With this course, I want to tell people about the tools, techniques and inside workings of this world of YouTube & Instagram videos. 

Engineering has taught me the importance of sharing crack codes to safely clear exams. Having found the crack code to content myself, the engineering kid in me can’t help but share it with others too.

No, this course is designed for beginners, and we'll cover the fundamentals before diving into advanced techniques.

The course is self-paced, allowing you to learn at your convenience. The course comes with lifetime access, so you can continue learning and revisiting the material whenever you need.

Hardware:
Intel i5 or i7 processor
Dedicated GPU (e.g., NVIDIA GeForce or AMD Radeon)
16GB of RAM for enhanced editing performance
